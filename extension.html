Stavich Bicycle Trail - Extensions of the Ride

A 1.9-mile loop between the trail and the Mahoning River offers a variation on the route. This road is named East River Rd where it crosses the trail at westbound MP4.2 and Hillsville Rd where it crosses the trail at MP5.7. Turn toward the river at either of these crossings; the road name changes where a side road crosses the river (to make the loop, do not cross the river). Note: In 6/2025 the pavement from MP 4.2 to the river was being worked on.should now be good. From MP5.7 to the river was okay.
